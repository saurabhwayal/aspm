Admissions Information

Admissions are open for the academic year 2026–27. Parents are requested to read the details carefully.

Eligibility

  • Pre-Primary: Minimum age 3 years
  • Primary: As per education board rules
  • Documents required during admission

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card (Student & Parent)
  • Previous School Leaving Certificate
  • Passport Size Photographs

Admission Process

  1. Collect admission form from school office
  2. Submit filled form with documents
  3. Interaction / Entrance (if applicable)
  4. Confirmation of admission
